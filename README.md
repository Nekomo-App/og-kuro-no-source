## 📖 About Kuro No

Kuro No (often called Kuro TV) was a popular anime application that allowed users to browse, track, and watch anime through various providers. Over the years, it built a dedicated community and inspired several forks and alternative projects. The original repository was maintained by Deceptions and gained popularity among Android and TV users.

Unfortunately, development appears to have slowed significantly, and many users have reported broken sources, missing functionality, and a lack of updates in recent years. Community discussions suggest that the original project is no longer actively maintained.

## 🐱 About Nekomo

Nekomo is a community project inspired by the legacy of Kuro No and Shiro.

The goal of Nekomo is not only to preserve parts of what made those projects special but also to provide a place where anime fans can gather, share resources, discuss anime, and exchange information about alternative applications and related projects.

At the moment, Nekomo is currently on hold and future development remains uncertain. The project may eventually receive updates, be rewritten from scratch, remain inactive, or be continued by new contributors.

Despite this, the community remains open to anime fans, developers, and anyone interested in helping preserve or improve projects inspired by Kuro No.

## 📢 Current Nekomo Status

> **Project Status: On Hold**

For now, Nekomo primarily serves as an anime community and resource hub.

## 🌟 Legacy

Without Kuro No, projects such as Shiro, Nekomo, and various community forks may never have existed.

While the future of these projects remains uncertain, this page exists to help preserve information about the original project and the communities that formed around it.
